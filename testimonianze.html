While staying in Alghero for a couple days, we dropped in for a spontaneous training session and were very warmly welcomed by the owners (we even got some great restaurant and sightseeing recommendations).
The gym is completely new and offers several brand new barbells, dumbbells, and weight racks for functional fitness or weightlifting / Crossfit as well as some older fitness machines that work perfectly well for training. The equipment offered includes rowing machine, treadmill, bicycle, leg press, various cable towers and other machines.
Overall, the place has a good mix of old and new and a very positive vibe, which is why I am happy to recommend FitStop and visit it again when I am in Alghero.
